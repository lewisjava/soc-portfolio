# MITRE D3FEND

Where MITRE ATT&CK teaches you how attacks happen, [D3FEND](https://d3fend.mitre.org/) teaches you how to discover and stop them.

**D3FEND** stands for **D**etection, **D**enial, and **D**isruption **F**ramework **E**mpowering **N**etwork **D**efence.

It's a structured framework that maps out defensive technology and establishes a common language for describing how security controls actually work — the natural counterpart to ATT&CK's offensive taxonomy.

![D3FEND matrix — defensive technique categories](/static/images/notes/threat-intel/d3fend-matrix-1.png)

![D3FEND matrix — technique detail view](/static/images/notes/threat-intel/d3fend-matrix-2.png)

Citing D3FEND technique IDs alongside ATT&CK technique IDs in a write-up or detection rule shows both sides of the picture — what the attacker technique was, and which category of defensive control actually catches it.
